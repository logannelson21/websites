• About Big-D Welding

I'm a crafty welder. I work out of my shop in my backyard and own all of the necessary equipment for welding. I have close to 60 years of experience. I specialize in making stoves and other convenience products, but I can do sepcial requests as well. I weld for fun and also sell various other things, including firewood.
